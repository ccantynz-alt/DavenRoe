# Astra — Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 2026**

## 1. Data We Collect

| Data Type | Purpose | Stored Where | Retention |
|-----------|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Account info (name, email) | User authentication | Astra database | Until account deletion |
| Business entity data | Accounting operations | Astra database | Until account deletion |
| Bank feed transactions | Automated bookkeeping | Astra database | 7 years (regulatory) |
| Invoices and documents | Record keeping | Astra database | 7 years (regulatory) |
| Payroll data | Tax compliance | Astra database | 7 years (regulatory) |
| AI interaction logs | Service improvement | Astra database | 90 days |

## 3. We Do NOT

- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your financial data for advertising
- Share data with AI training datasets
- Store bank login credentials (only tokenized access)
- Access your bank accounts for any purpose other than read-only feed ingestion

## 4. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:
- **Access** your data (data export available)
- **Delete** your data (account deletion removes all non-regulatory data)
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Port** your data to another service (CSV/JSON export)
- **Object** to AI processing (manual-only mode available)
- **Restrict** processing in specific circumstances

## 5. Jurisdictional Compliance

- **Australia**: Privacy Act 1988, APPs. Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.
- **New Zealand**: Privacy Act 2020. Privacy Commissioner notification.
- **United States**: Applicable state laws (CCPA, etc.). SOC 2 compliance target.
- **United Kingdom**: UK GDPR, Data Protection Act 2018. ICO registration.

## 6. Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach:
1. Affected users notified within 72 hours
2. Relevant regulatory authority notified as required by jurisdiction
3. Forensic investigation initiated
4. Remediation measures communicated
