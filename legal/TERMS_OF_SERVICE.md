# Astra — Terms of Service

**Last Updated: March 2026**

## 1. Nature of Service

Astra is an AI-assisted accounting **tool**, not an accounting firm, tax advisor, or
financial institution. Astra does not provide professional accounting advice,
tax advice, legal advice, or financial advice.

## 2. AI-Generated Output Disclaimer

### 2.1 Human Review Required
All AI-generated outputs — including but not limited to transaction categorizations,
financial narratives, tax calculations, forensic analysis findings, and due diligence
reports — are **drafts requiring human review and approval** before any reliance or action.

### 2.2 No Guarantee of Accuracy
While Astra's tax engine uses deterministic logic derived from published legislation,
tax law is complex and subject to interpretation. AI-generated categorizations and
narratives are probabilistic and may contain errors. **You are responsible for verifying
all outputs before use.**

### 2.3 Not a Substitute for Professional Advice
Astra does not replace the need for a qualified accountant, tax advisor, or auditor.
For material financial decisions, tax filings, regulatory compliance, or legal matters,
you must consult with appropriately licensed professionals.

## 5. Forensic Accounting Module

### 5.1 Investigative Tool, Not Legal Evidence
Forensic analysis outputs are **investigative aids**, not legal evidence. Findings
must be independently verified by qualified forensic accountants before use in
any legal, regulatory, or transactional context.

### 5.2 No Fraud Guarantee
Astra's forensic engines use statistical methods (Benford's Law, anomaly detection,
pattern matching) that can indicate but cannot prove fraud. The absence of flags
does not guarantee the absence of fraud or irregularities.

### 5.3 M&A Due Diligence Limitation
The "90-Minute Financial Health Audit" is a screening tool. It does not replace
comprehensive due diligence by qualified professionals. Acquisition decisions should
never be made solely on Astra's output.

## 6. Data Privacy and Security

### 6.1 Bank Feed Data

under their respective terms. Astra does not store bank credentials.

### 6.2 AI Processing
Transaction data may be processed by third-party AI providers (Anthropic) for
categorization and narrative generation. Data is not used for AI model training.

### 6.3 Jurisdictional Compliance
Data handling complies with applicable data protection laws including GDPR (EU/UK),
Privacy Act 1988 (AU), Privacy Act 2020 (NZ), and applicable US state privacy laws.
